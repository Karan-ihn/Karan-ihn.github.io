Karan Chouhan

Hi, I'm Karan Chouhan, currently a student of the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IITB), pursuing a B.Tech degree in Metallurgical Engineering and Material Science department.

I just love to make new friends and to know different people and their tastes, thus IITB have provided me such a huge number of opportunities to do this. Apart from this I also participated and enjoy various competitions like XLR8, Tinkerer's projects, Enigma crypt hunt, competitive coding competitions etc which all help me to relieve the academic stress. I'm currently a first year student which is going to be promoted to second year. A amazing year passed with all the joy and craziness.
